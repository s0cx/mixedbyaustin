About

Hey — I'm Austin Simard, a mix engineer and producer based in Vancouver, BC.

I work with pop and hip-hop artists to shape records that feel human, dynamic, and intentional. I’m obsessed with sonics that hit emotionally as much as they hit technically.

Half my brain is tuned to frequencies and phase; the other half’s tuned to storytelling, vibe, and pacing. My favorite moments in music are the ones where something clicks unexpectedly — a feeling gets revealed that wasn’t there before. That’s the kind of work I aim to do with every artist I mix or produce for.

If you want to send me demos or just talk shop, I’m always open. You can email me or DM me on Instagram.
