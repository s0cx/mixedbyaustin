About

I'm Austin — I mix and produce music from my setup in Vancouver. Mostly pop and hip-hop, but I like working on anything that feels intentional.

I try to keep things sounding human, clean, and a little unexpected. I care about detail, emotion, and how a mix moves. Not just technically — but how it makes someone *feel* on the first listen.

If you're curious about working together or just want to send something, reach out. Email works. Instagram DMs too.
